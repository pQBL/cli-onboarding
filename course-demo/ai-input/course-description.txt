This course is for introducing new employees at the Division of Media Technology
and Interaction Design at the Swedish Royal Institute of Technology (KTH).

Assume that the learners are highly educated.

It should contain about 10 questions, that focus on what the department is
doing.

Try to cover all the available areas:

- Creative Media Technology
- Interaction Design
- Media Lab
- Sound and Music Computing
- SPACE - Social, Physical, and Cultural Environment
- Sustainable Futures Lab
- Technology Enhanced Learning
